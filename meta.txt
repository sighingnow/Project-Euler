The algorithm, program and answer of problems in project euler.

All solutions include the problem description in english and chinese, the answer *Accept* on

### What's project-euler ?

[Project euler](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_Euler "Project Euler")(named after
Leonhard Euler) is a website dedicated to a series of computational problems intended to be
solved with computer programs. The project attracts adults and students interested in
mathematics and computer programming.
